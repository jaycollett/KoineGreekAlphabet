How It Works

Your Progress is Adaptive

After 10 quizzes, the app intelligently focuses on your weak spots. 60% of questions target letters you struggle with, while 40% cover all letters for retention and balanced practice.

Your mastery score combines overall accuracy, current correct streak, and consistency over time. Regular practice improves retention and builds lasting recognition.
